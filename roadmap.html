Users can participate in contests and promotions of the project on atomichub.

You can collect all 2/2 promo NFT and in turn, exchange all 2 cards for 1/1 of a limited secret raccoon.

And also collect a whitelist using discord, inviting 40 friends and getting level 5 in the chat!

Just be sure to perform all actions very honestly and without cheating! We will be obliged to check all invited friends

Buying packs of raccoons //

The second stage of the project is the sale of packs, where everyone can purchase one or more sets of our project.

There will be two purchase options, a whitelist sale and a sale for everyone who wants to purchase limited packs.

Be sure to keep the Premium PASS on your account before buying through the whitelist!

Also keep in mind that all possible whitelist accounts will be entered and cashed 15 minutes before the public sale!

You can open card sets exactly 2 hours after the sale of packs Mini pack and Big pack.

Getting bonuses //

All holders of our NFTs will receive rewards every 10th day depending on the rarity of your NFT

Comonn - $600 for all holders.

Rare - $1000 for all holders.

Epic - $3000 for all holders.

Legends - $5000 for all holders.

Secret.Raccoon - $1000 for all holders.

How it will happen:

On the morning of the 10th day, we make a snapshot of all the holders of our NFT cards, and add them to the list of receiving awards.

The awards will be sent later in the evening of the same day. The order of distribution will depend on the number and rarity of your NFTs.

Note: If you own the highest NFT rarity, then with the further distribution of awards, you will be the first in the list of receiving a bonus.

Sending funds to the fund //

At the end of each month, we will send all proceeds from sales of our collection from the secondary market at https://www.ifaw.org/eu.

Namely, 6% of the commission from the sale / purchase on the secondary market, which come to the author of the collection.

Sending collectible merch

Every two months we will send a collectible merch to the top 20 buyers of our collection.

The merch will be limited, that is, it will be updated every two months.

Note: We ship independently to any country. Delivery is carried out at the expense of our collection.

Sending bonuses to whitelist holders //

At the beginning of each month, we randomly select 10 random premium pass holders who will receive exclusive gifts from our collection.

To get a premium pass, you need to invite 40 friends to our discord server and get the 5th level there.

Also keep in mind that we will check every user you invite for spam and multiaccounting options.

Note:
We hope that you will be honest with our collection.
